Write a note.

Seal a note, a gesture, or a small gift for a future day — for yourself, or for someone you love. On a hard day, past-you shows up with exactly what you needed to hear.

Write your first capsule See how it works
1 · Seal

Write what's true today

A letter to future-you. A note for a friend. A small gesture. A gift you pledge. Add the context of the moment you're writing from — it becomes the most valuable part.

2 · Set

Pick when it arrives

Tomorrow, next birthday, the morning after a hard anniversary. We hold it until the exact moment, then deliver by email.

3 · Compound

Small investments, real returns

Time, emotion, and care compound like anything else. The gesture you seal today is worth more when it arrives.

One day you'll be having a terrible day and get a note from you in the past that you forgot about — and that moment will put into context how bad times pass and investments compound.

Gestures & prompts

"Call Mom." "Take the long way home." "Put on that song." Small pointers your future self will thank you for.

Micro gifts (pledge)

Pledge a small amount — for yourself, or to pay forward. The delivery carries the pledge and the note that matters more.
